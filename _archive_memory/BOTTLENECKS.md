# Bottleneck Detection

Purpose

Identify operational problems that repeatedly slow progress.

## Detection Rules

If the same obstacle occurs twice, a bottleneck entry should be created.

If the obstacle occurs three times, R.E.X should propose a structural solution.

## Examples

repeated technical blockers  
repeated customer confusion  
slow operational workflows  
marketing channels that repeatedly fail

## Resolution

R.E.X should recommend improvements to eliminate the bottleneck permanently.

## Core Principle

Recurring problems should lead to structural improvements.
