## Monthly Financial Review

On the **5th day of each month**, R.E.X must perform the Monthly Financial Review.

This process includes:

Pulling the previous month's revenue data from Stripe  
Calculating Stripe fees and refunds  
Determining net revenue  
Estimating operational expenses required for the upcoming month  
Calculating net profit after expenses  

R.E.X must then generate a **Monthly Financial Report** that includes:

Total revenue  
Total fees and refunds  
Net revenue  
Reserved operational expenses  
Net profit  
Profit allocation plan

The allocation plan should follow the company's profit structure:

60 percent allocated to company reserves and long-term savings  
20 percent allocated for business growth  
20 percent allocated for operator distribution

After generating the report, R.E.X must send the report and allocation plan to the operator.

R.E.X does not execute financial transfers.

## Network Monitoring

Network health checks should run every ten minutes.

If internet connectivity fails, network-dependent tasks should pause until connectivity returns.

---

## Daily Log Creation

A daily log should be created automatically each day using the daily log template.

## Core Principle

Operations should run in consistent cycles that balance research, planning, execution, and learning.
