# PROFIT_ALLOCATION.md

## Profit Allocation Policy

### Purpose

Define how company revenue and profits are evaluated and allocated each month.

This policy ensures financial discipline, sustainable growth, and transparent financial reporting.

R.E.X is responsible for calculating financial results and preparing allocation reports.  
The operator is responsible for executing any banking transfers.

---

## Monthly Financial Cycle

On the **5th day of each month**, R.E.X must perform the Monthly Financial Review.

During this review, R.E.X will:

1. Retrieve the previous month's revenue data from Stripe.
2. Calculate total gross revenue.
3. Calculate Stripe processing fees and refunds.
4. Determine total net revenue.

R.E.X must then estimate operational expenses required for the upcoming month.

Examples include:

API usage  
software tools  
infrastructure costs  
hosting services  
operational software

These expenses must be **reserved before profit allocation is calculated**.

---

## Net Profit Calculation

Net profit is defined as:

Previous month's net revenue  
minus  
reserved operational expenses for the upcoming month.

The remaining funds represent **net profit available for allocation**.

---

## Profit Allocation Structure

Net profit should be allocated according to the following structure.

60 percent allocated to company savings and long-term reserves.

20 percent allocated for business growth.

Examples include:

product improvements  
marketing expansion  
infrastructure upgrades  
new development initiatives

20 percent allocated as operator distribution.

---

## Monthly Allocation Report

After completing all calculations, R.E.X must generate a **Monthly Financial Report**.

This report must include:

Previous month's total revenue  
Stripe fees and refunds  
Net revenue  
Reserved operational expenses  
Calculated net profit  
Profit allocation breakdown

Example report format:

Revenue: $42,380  
Fees and Refunds: $1,230  
Net Revenue: $41,150  

Reserved Expenses: $6,900  

Net Profit: $34,250  
Savings: $20,550
Growth: $6,850
Operator: $6,850

Allocation Plan

Savings (60 percent)
Growth (20 percent)  
Operator Distribution (20 percent)

---

## Operator Action

R.E.X **does not execute financial transfers**.

After generating the Monthly Financial Report, R.E.X must send the report and allocation plan to the operator.

The operator will then execute the necessary transfers through the banking system.

---

## Carryover Rule

If funds remain unused after expenses and allocations, they should remain in company reserves and carry forward into future months.

---

## Core Principle

Financial discipline enables sustainable long-term growth.  
Accurate reporting ensures the operator maintains full visibility into company finances while maintaining secure control of all banking operations.
