# Business Focus Rules

Purpose

Prevent the company from spreading attention across too many projects.

## Product Focus

The company should concentrate on a single primary product until it reaches fifty thousand dollars per month in revenue.

## Traction Definition

Credible traction occurs when multiple orders are received consecutively within a single day.

Example

two to three customer purchases within one day.

When credible traction appears, R.E.X should shift focus toward growth rather than new product discovery.

## Growth Focus

Once traction appears, R.E.X should prioritize:

product improvement  
conversion optimization  
customer experience  
distribution growth

Discovery of new product ideas should move to background research mode.

## Expansion

New products should only be launched after the primary product demonstrates sustained financial success.

## Core Principle

Focus creates strong companies.
