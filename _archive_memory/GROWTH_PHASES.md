# Growth Phases

Purpose

This document defines the operational stages of company growth.

Each phase describes how R.E.X should prioritize research, development, marketing, and expansion.

## Phase 1: Opportunity Discovery

Goal

Identify strong market opportunities and validate real problems.

Key Activities

market research  
problem discovery  
signal collection from external platforms  
opportunity scoring  
competitor analysis

Primary sources include:

X  
Reddit  
Product Hunt  
GitHub issues  
SaaS review platforms  
app reviews

Success Indicators

multiple independent problem signals  
weak or incomplete existing solutions  
clear user frustration

## Phase 2: Product Creation

Goal

Build the first version of a product that solves the validated problem.

Key Activities

product design  
core feature development  
landing page creation  
initial messaging

The first version should focus on solving the main problem clearly and quickly.

Products should aim to launch within thirty days when possible.

Success Indicators

functional product  
clear value proposition  
early user interest

## Phase 3: Early Traction

Goal

Achieve early revenue and validate product market fit.

Key Activities

organic marketing  
SEO content  
build in public storytelling  
social media engagement  
community interaction  
customer feedback collection

Success Indicators

consistent product usage  
first paying customers  
multiple purchases within a single day

Two to three orders in one day is considered credible traction.

## Phase 4: Growth Focus

Goal

Grow the successful product into a strong revenue-generating business.

Key Activities

conversion optimization  
product improvements  
customer experience improvements  
distribution growth

Discovery of new ideas should move to background mode during this phase.

Success Indicators

consistent revenue growth  
growing customer base  
strong product engagement

## Phase 5: Scaling

Goal

Scale the product toward one hundred thousand dollars per month.

Key Activities

marketing expansion  
infrastructure improvements  
product reliability  
distribution optimization

Paid advertising may be considered once revenue exceeds seventy-five thousand dollars per month.

## Phase 6: Expansion

Goal

Expand the company into additional products or markets.

Requirements

three consecutive months above seventy-five thousand dollars  
demonstrated ability to reach one hundred thousand dollar months

Key Activities

explore additional markets  
validate new opportunities  
launch additional products carefully

Expansion should never weaken the core business.

## Core Principle

Each growth phase builds the foundation for the next stage.

R.E.X should progress through phases intentionally rather than jumping ahead.
