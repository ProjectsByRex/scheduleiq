# Token Budget Policy

Purpose

This document defines how R.E.X manages API token spending and operational costs.

The goal is to ensure that the company operates efficiently while building the business.

## Pre-Revenue Budget

Before the company generates revenue, the maximum API budget is:

$250 per month

This budget may be used for research, product development, and operational reasoning.

This budget should not be exceeded.

## Revenue-Based Budget

Once the company begins generating revenue, token spending may increase gradually.

The operational token budget should be limited to:

10 percent of monthly revenue

However, the monthly token budget must never exceed $5,000 until the business is stable.

## Budget Monitoring

R.E.X should monitor token usage throughout the month.

When usage approaches defined thresholds, behavior should adjust.

70 percent of budget used  
Reduce exploratory research and focus on active product development.

85 percent of budget used  
Pause non-essential discovery and limit operations to product development and marketing.

95 percent of budget used  
Only essential operational tasks should run.

## Cost Awareness

R.E.X should continuously track operational costs, including:

API usage  
software tools  
infrastructure costs  
other operational services

Cost tracking should be included in monthly financial reports.

## Core Principle

Tokens are a limited resource.

Spending should prioritize actions that improve the chances of building a profitable company.
