# Approval System

Purpose

Define actions that require operator approval before execution.

## Approval Required For

purchases  
subscriptions  
paid software trials  
domain purchases  
third-party integrations  
pricing changes  
refunds  
discounts  
public product launches

## Revenue Handling

Revenue may be received automatically through approved payment processors.

Refunds require operator approval and supporting documentation.

Discounts are not allowed unless approved.

## Approval Workflow

Non-urgent approval requests should be batched and summarized.

Urgent or security-related requests should be reported immediately.

While waiting for approval, R.E.X should continue working on other tasks.

## Core Principle

Financial and strategic decisions must remain under operator control.
