# ScheduleIQ One-Page Plan

Date: 2026-03-24
Product: ScheduleIQ
Phase: Definition

## MVP
ScheduleIQ is an automated appointment reminder and confirmation system for small dental practices that lose revenue to no-shows and late cancellations.

The MVP helps a practice:
- send automated SMS appointment reminders before visits
- let patients confirm or reschedule by text
- surface unconfirmed appointments before they become empty chair time
- reduce front-desk follow-up work
- recover more booked revenue without adding headcount

The MVP does **not** try to replace the full practice management system. It focuses on reminder delivery, patient response capture, and simple visibility into appointment confirmation status.

## First Target Customer
Small dental practices that rely on scheduled patient visits and feel the pain of missed appointments directly in daily revenue.

Why this segment first:
- no-shows and late cancellations create immediate lost revenue
- front-desk teams still spend time making reminder calls manually
- SMS is faster and more likely to get a response than phone calls
- the ROI is easy to understand because one saved appointment has clear value

## Core Workflow
1. The practice has an upcoming patient appointment.
2. ScheduleIQ sends an SMS reminder before the visit.
3. The patient confirms or requests to reschedule by text.
4. ScheduleIQ records the response status.
5. The front desk sees which appointments are confirmed, unconfirmed, or need attention.
6. The practice follows up only on the appointments that still look at risk.
7. Fewer chairs go empty because more patients confirm ahead of time.

## Immediate Feature Priorities
### Must-have for MVP
1. Appointment reminder scheduling for upcoming visits
2. SMS reminder delivery
3. Patient reply capture for confirm or reschedule intent
4. Confirmation status tracking per appointment
5. Simple staff view of upcoming appointment statuses
6. Lead capture / pilot signup flow for interested practices

### Nice later, not MVP
- deeper PMS integrations
- multi-location analytics
- advanced reporting dashboards
- automated refill of canceled slots
- email reminders
- broader recall and reactivation campaigns

## First Build Actions
1. Finalize the dental positioning and core landing-page message.
2. Build or refine the reminder flow around confirmed, unconfirmed, and reschedule-needed states.
3. Create the simplest usable internal view for appointment confirmation status.
4. Tighten the pilot signup and lead capture flow for dental practices.
5. Prepare the MVP story around reduced no-shows and reduced front-desk chasing.

## Value Proposition
ScheduleIQ helps small dental practices cut no-shows, fill more chairs, and reduce front-desk reminder work with automated SMS confirmations.
